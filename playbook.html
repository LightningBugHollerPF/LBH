Gaming
Play without a console · or plug in your own
📍 3rd Floor · Game Room
▼
🎮 No console? No problem. The game room TV is a Samsung QN-Q7FA QLED with Samsung Gaming Hub built right in. Connect a Bluetooth controller, sign into Xbox Game Pass or GeForce Now, and start playing — nothing to plug in or download.

Option 1 — Play via Gaming Hub (no console needed)

  • 1Press Home on the TV remote and select Gaming Hub from the menu.
  • 2Inside Gaming Hub, select the controller icon and put your controller into Bluetooth pairing mode. PS4, PS5 DualSense, and Xbox controllers all pair directly.
  • 3Choose Xbox Game Pass or GeForce Now and sign in with your existing account.
  • 4Pick a game and play — everything streams over WiFi, nothing downloads.
📋 What you need: Xbox Game Pass Ultimate (~$15/mo) gives access to 400+ games. GeForce Now has a free tier — or paid tiers for 4K. Both work with PlayStation and Xbox controllers.

Option 2 — Bring your own console (PS4/PS5, Xbox, Switch)

  • 1Plug an HDMI cable from your console into any open HDMI IN port on the back of the TV. Note the port number.
  • 2Power on both. The TV usually auto-switches — if not, press Source (top-left of remote) and pick the right HDMI input.
  • 3When done, press Source and select Xumo to return to streaming.
🔧 Black screen after plugging in? Press Source and confirm the correct HDMI number is selected. Pressing the PS/Xbox button on your controller often triggers auto-detection.

Popcorn Maker
Hot air · no oil needed · use ½ bag per session
📍 3rd Floor · Game Room
▼

Nostalgia Vintage Popcorn Maker · NHAP501

Popcorn bags are provided — use ½ of one bag per session (fill only to the line inside the measuring cup).

  • 1Place on a flat surface. Attach Top Housing to Base.
  • 2Place a large bowl under the Popcorn Chute.
  • 3Remove Measuring Cup. Switch ON. Heat for 3 minutes.
  • 4Switch OFF. Fill cup to the line only — do not overfill.
  • 5Pour into Popping Chamber, replace cup, switch back ON.
  • 6Popcorn bursts into the bowl within minutes!
  • 7When slowing to 1–2 pops/second, turn OFF. Cool 10 min before next batch.
⚠️ Important: Do NOT put oil, butter, or anything other than kernels into the Popping Chamber. Add toppings to the bowl afterward.
🍿 Seasoning tip: Spritz with olive oil so seasonings stick. Try parmesan + garlic, or cinnamon sugar for sweet kettle corn.
🔧 Smoke or burning? Unplug immediately — chamber was overfilled. Cool fully before trying again.

Furnishings & Extras

Sleeper Sofa
Flip-over bed · clears in 60 seconds · no cushion removal
▼

Latitude Run® Monreal 72.9″ Round Arm Sofa Bed · Owner Manual (Wayfair)

🛋️ Sofa → Bed

  • 1Clear the area — you'll need 5–6 feet of open floor space in front of the sofa. The whole frame unfolds forward.
  • 2Find the hidden handle — reach behind the top center of the backrest cushions and grip the fabric loop or handle integrated into the frame.
  • 3Initiate the flip — pull the backrest toward you and downward. You're rotating the entire back assembly forward over the seat cushions, not just folding it.
  • 4Guide the frame — as the backrest rotates forward, the seat cushions tuck underneath and the metal bed frame extends. Keep a steady grip to control the descent.
  • 5Lower to the floor — continue the rotation until the bed frame legs meet the floor. The backrest cushions are now face-down, forming the foot-end support.
  • 6Add the pillows and sheets we provide — bed is ready.
💡 First time? The mechanism is spring-assisted — once you get past the midpoint of the flip, the frame lowers itself. Don't fight it, just guide it down slowly.

🌅 Bed → Sofa

  • 1Remove all bedding and pillows.
  • 2Lift from the foot of the bed — grip the frame at the far end and push up and back.
  • 3The mechanism tucks itself back — guide it upright until the backrest locks into sofa position.
🔧 Frame feels heavy coming up? Make sure all bedding is removed first — extra weight fights the spring assist. Use both hands and push steadily rather than in one fast motion.
